Visual Exploration

This page features selected photography and visual studies that serve as a creative outlet alongside my UX and development work. Exploring visuals outside of interfaces helps inform how I approach composition, color, and storytelling in digital design.

Transition

A portrait capturing a moment of change, using space and perspective to emphasize movement and personal growth during a transitional milestone. Photo taken by Me.

Duality

A visual exploration of identity and environment using double exposure to merge human form with natural landscapes. Photo taken and edited by Me with Adobe Photoshop.

Disconnection

An exploration of modern social interaction, highlighting how digital devices influence presence, attention, and collective behavior. Photo taken by Me.

Reflection

A symmetrical composition examining self-perception through duplication, using minimal elements and high-exposure lighting to create abstraction. Photo taken by Me.
